Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard in-stock items are delivered within 2–4 weeks. Custom pieces typically require 8–14 weeks.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes — complimentary white-glove delivery on all orders over $1,500 within the contiguous United States.

Can I customize pieces?

Absolutely. Most pieces are available in a range of fabrics, leathers, wood stains, and finishes.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns on standard items within 30 days of delivery, provided the piece is in original condition.

What does the warranty cover?

Our 10-year warranty covers structural defects in frames, spring systems, and hardware.

Do you offer design consultations?

Yes — our consultants are available for virtual or in-store consultations. Sessions are free of charge.
