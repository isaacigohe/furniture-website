Our Story

A Legacy of Exceptional Furniture

LuxeHome was born from a simple but powerful belief: that the furniture in your home should be as remarkable as the life you live within it.

Founded in 2008 in a small Vermont workshop, LuxeHome began with a team of five craftsmen and a commitment to making furniture the right way — with solid materials, honest construction, and thoughtful design.

In the years since, we have grown into a nationally recognized brand with showrooms across the country, yet our core philosophy has never wavered. Today, LuxeHome employs over 200 craftsmen, designers, and specialists who share a singular passion: creating furniture that stands apart.

We are not simply a furniture store. We are the curators of your home's story — and we take that responsibility seriously.

The Founder

Elias Wren — Master Craftsman

Elias Wren grew up in a family of woodworkers in rural Vermont, where his grandfather's workshop was the center of the household. By age twelve, he had built his first chair.

After apprenticing under master cabinetmakers in Denmark and Japan, Elias returned to Vermont with a vision: to bring the quality and artistry of the world's finest furniture workshops to American homes.

"I have always believed that a great piece of furniture is not merely an object — it is a relationship. Between the craftsman and the wood, between the designer and the space, between the piece and the person who lives with it every day."

Today, Elias remains deeply involved in LuxeHome's design process, personally reviewing every new collection.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Uncompromising Quality

We never cut corners. Every piece meets the same rigorous standards whether it is a $500 side table or a $15,000 dining suite.

Honest Craftsmanship

We use real materials — solid wood, genuine leather, natural stone — and we are transparent about what goes into every piece.

Thoughtful Design

Beauty and function are not competing values — they are partners. Our designers create pieces that are visually stunning and ergonomically excellent.

Environmental Responsibility

We source materials sustainably and design furniture that lasts for generations — because the most sustainable piece is one that never needs replacing.

Customer Partnership

We see every customer as a long-term partner. From the first consultation to final delivery, we are committed to an exceptional experience.

Community & Legacy

We invest in the communities where we operate, supporting apprenticeship programs and preserving traditional woodworking skills.
